About Me

Hello! I'm Jordan, a Software Developer based in Eau Claire, WI who enjoys building tools that make life a little easier. I develop websites and web apps that present data with an intuitive user-friendly design.

After graduating from the University of Wisconsin - Eau Claire, I joined the Point of Sale team at Menards where I work on the whole stack, advancing old technologies to new hip ones.

What sparked the interest?

The first game I created was a puzzle that was created in a 300+ slide show on PowerPoint. It was self initiated and one of my more memorable projects. The game utilized internal hyperlinks, that when touched with the mouse a life would be lost. Each slide had a different maze layout that contained locked doors and various obstacles. After copying the PowerPoint game on a flash drive and sharing with many, it was a done deal, I knew I wanted to continue to work with computers. I enjoy creating stuff that puts a smile on someone's face. I was in 8th grade at the time and it still holds true.
